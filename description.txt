### Item Stats+
Item Stats+ will display more in-depth stats for all weapons.
Three diferent stlyes can be used

### Changelog

v1.2.2:
- You can click "Off", "On', and "Alt" to set all.
- Added "#" option to knockback and speed.

v1.2:
- Now saves!
- Improvements to GUI.

v1.1:
- Added GUI!

v1.0:
- Release!
